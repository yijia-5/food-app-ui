Tomato Bruschetta

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 5 mins | 4 servings
(128 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 6 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 French baguette, toasted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, basil, garlic, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Allow mixture to sit for 10 minutes for flavors to blend.
  3. Brush baguette slices with olive oil and toast under a broiler until lightly browned.
  4. Top each slice with tomato mixture.
  5. Optionally sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
